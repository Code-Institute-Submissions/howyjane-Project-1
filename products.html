Gold ETFs

A Gold ETF is an exchange-traded fund (ETF) that aims to track the domestic physical gold price. They are passive investment instruments that are based on gold prices and invest in gold bullion.

Buying gold ETFs means you are purchasing gold in an electronic form. You can buy and sell gold ETFs just as you would trade in stocks. When you actually redeem Gold ETF, you donâ€™t get physical gold but receive the cash equivalent. Trading of gold ETFs takes place through a dematerialized account (Demat) and a broker, which makes it an extremely convenient way of electronically investing in gold.

Advantages

Gold ETFs provide investors with a low-cost, diversified alternative that invests in gold-backed assets rather than the physical commodity.

Gold ETFs may have management fees and significant tax implications for long-term investors.
